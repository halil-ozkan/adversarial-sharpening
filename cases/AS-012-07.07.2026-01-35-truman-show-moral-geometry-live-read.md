## Core Claim

This artifact demonstrates attentive live moral-geometry reading.

In the vaguest first act of *The Truman Show*, the read identified the emerging control system before the film fully made it explicit: desire appears, perception notices cracks, and the world responds through containment, distraction, gaslighting, commerce, fear, or choreography.

This is not a complete film audit. It is a reproducible record of live ethical pattern detection.

## Method

1. Watch live without pausing for formal research.
2. Capture timestamps when moral pressure appears.
3. Separate direct observation from interpretation.
4. Name the moral axis involved.
5. Upgrade confidence only when later scenes support earlier suspicions.
6. Do not polish during capture.
7. Afterward, select the strongest evidence chains.
